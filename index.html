Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty, a monumental gift from France, has graced New York Harbor since 1886. Designed by Frédéric Auguste Bartholdi, its majestic figure symbolizes freedom, democracy, and the pursuit of a better life. With a torch held high, it radiates light, guiding immigrants arriving by sea to a land of opportunity. The statue's crown features seven rays representing the continents. Standing on Liberty Island, it witnesses historic moments and embraces diverse cultures.
